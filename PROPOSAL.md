# What are you building? One or two sentences describing your project.
I am building a Menu Decoder. It will take text input from any restaurant menu and analyze it to decipher any possible allergen concerns, dietary restrictions, cuisine origins, average price, nd more restaurant details.

# Why? What motivates you to build this? What problem does it solve?
My mom is pescatarian, meaning the only meat she eats if fish. When we go out to eat as a family, we try to find restaurants with multiple vegetarian or fish-based meals so that she has different options to choose from, but menus can be time consuming to review manually. This project would help people with similar needs who are looking for a way to understand a restaurant's offerings with greater efficiency. It will also help people looking to reduce spending by signifying higher prices, and will help foodies who are interested in learning more about what they eat.

# MVP vs. Stretch Goals: What is the minimum working version? What would you add if you have time?
MVP:
- copy and paste text menu from restaurant website
- extract dish names, major ingredients, prices 
- flag common allergens
- identify cuisine origins keywords
- display information analyzed
Stretch Goals:
- menu scoring
- price visualizations
- analyzing images or pdfs, rather than text input
- compare multiple menus
